Why Location Matters for Appliance Efficiency

🌱 Grid Composition Impact

States with renewable energy show 40-60% lower appliance CO₂ emissions. California's solar and wind reduce refrigerator impact from 280 to 180 lbs CO₂/year.

💰 Regional Cost Variations

Electricity pricing varies 3x across states. The same efficient appliance costs $45/year in Louisiana but $156/year in Hawaii - affecting payback periods.

🎯 Efficiency ROI by Region

High-emission states benefit most from efficiency upgrades. A 20% improvement saves 60 lbs CO₂/year in Texas vs 36 lbs in Washington.
